Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026
At Scankaro Helpkaro, your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our QR-based emergency and safety services.
1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address
  • Emergency Details: Contacts, medical information (if provided)
  • QR Usage Data: Scan activity, location (only when required for emergency access)
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, device type
2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide emergency contact access via QR scan
  • Enable instant owner identification
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Ensure security and fraud prevention
  • Communicate important updates
3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data.

Your information is only shared:

  • When a QR code is scanned in an emergency situation
  • With authorized emergency viewers (limited access)
  • When required by law or legal process
4. Data Security

We implement strong security measures:

  • Encrypted data storage
  • Secure access controls
  • Limited exposure of sensitive details

Your private information is never publicly visible without controlled access.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Update or correct information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Control what information is shared

To request changes, contact us at: support@scankaro.com

6. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies to:

  • Improve website performance
  • Analyze usage trends
  • Enhance user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools (analytics, hosting, etc.). These providers follow standard data protection policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy:

support@scankaro.com www.scankaro.com
Your safety is our priority. Your privacy is our responsibility.
