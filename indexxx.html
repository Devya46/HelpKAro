Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QR Safety System

What is QR Safety System?
QR Safety System allows you to attach a QR code to your Car, Bike, Pet, or Luggage. When someone scans it, they can instantly view emergency details and contact the owner.
How does QR code help in emergencies?
In case of accidents, wrong parking, or lost items, anyone can scan the QR code and directly connect with the owner or emergency contact without searching for numbers.
Where can I use this QR system?
You can use QR codes on Cars, Bikes, Pet collars, and Luggage to ensure quick identification and contact in any situation.
Is my personal information सुरक्षित (safe)?
Yes, your data is secure. Only limited information is shown during scanning, and sensitive details remain protected.
Do I need any app to scan the QR?
No app is required. Any smartphone camera can scan the QR code and open the details instantly.
